About the Doctor

A physician who
truly listens

Dr. Ayush Sharma is a qualified family physician (MBBS) based in Lower Oshiwara, Andheri West. With over a decade of practice, he is known in the neighbourhood for his patient, thorough approach to care — taking time to understand not just symptoms, but the full context of a patient's life.

His clinic is a warm, unhurried space where patients of all ages — from newborns to the elderly — feel heard. Whether it's a routine check-up or managing a long-term condition, Dr. Sharma's commitment is to lasting health.
